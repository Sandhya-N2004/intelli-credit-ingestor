Project Overview

The system performs credit risk analysis using multiple data sources and signals.

Financial Risk Prediction
A Random Forest model trained on corporate financial indicators predicts financial instability.

Fraud Detection
An Isolation Forest model detects anomalous or suspicious transaction activity.

Automated Secondary Research
The system crawls Google News to collect company-related news and performs sentiment analysis.

Risk Signal Detection
Promoter governance risk detection
Sector headwind detection
Litigation risk detection

Primary Insight Integration
The system allows credit officers to input qualitative observations such as operational issues or delayed payments.

Final Risk Scoring
All signals are combined to produce a final credit risk classification.
