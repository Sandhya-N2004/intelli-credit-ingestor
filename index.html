The Pipeline

Three stages.
One verdict.

01
Ingest & Normalize

Raw financial documents are uploaded and parsed. Tables are extracted, figures normalized, and data quality issues flagged before analysis begins.

02
Agent Orchestration

The Credit Manager Agent breaks the appraisal into sub-tasks — ratio analysis, trend detection, narrative interpretation — delegated to specialized sub-agents.

03
Score & Classify

Weighted scoring across dimensions produces a credit grade. Confidence intervals and sensitivity scenarios are computed automatically.

04
Recommend & Export

Actionable credit recommendations are generated and bundled into a structured report for analyst review or direct system integration.

Architecture

Three intelligent modules,
one unified system.

📥
Data Ingestor

The ingestion layer handles raw document parsing and standardization. Supports scanned PDFs via OCR, CSV balance sheets, and XML financial feeds. Outputs clean, structured JSON for downstream agents.

An LLM-orchestrated agent that plans and executes the credit appraisal workflow. Delegates tasks to sub-agents for ratio computation, peer benchmarking, and narrative risk flags — then synthesizes a final score.
